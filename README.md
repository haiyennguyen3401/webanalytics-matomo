## 🌊 Divezone — Web Analytics & Attribution Analysis with Matomo

This project presents a complete web analytics audit and digital marketing performance analysis for **Divezone**, an e-commerce company specialized in diving equipment.

Using Matomo, we analyzed more than **1.3 million website visits** and evaluated the effectiveness of the company’s acquisition channels including:

* Social media
* Search engines (SEA)
* Partner websites
* Retargeting opportunities

The objective of the project was to identify:

* The most profitable acquisition channels
* Conversion issues and abandoned carts
* Attribution model limitations
* Opportunities to improve marketing ROI

---

### 🔍 Key Topics Covered

* Traffic acquisition analysis
* ROAS & KPI measurement
* Multi-touch attribution models
* Funnel analysis
* Cart abandonment analysis
* SEO / SEA performance
* Social media contribution
* Retargeting & email marketing strategy
* Budget reallocation recommendations

---

### 📊 Main Insights

* 1.3M annual visits generated $5.3M in revenue
* 85% of carts were abandoned
* Search engines generated high traffic but low-quality conversions
* Partner websites delivered strong ROAS
* Social media channels played different roles across the customer journey
* Last-click attribution underestimated awareness channels

---

### ✅ Recommendations

* Implement a multi-touch attribution model in Matomo
* Optimize Google Ads keywords and landing pages
* Increase investment in high-performing channels such as Reddit
* Launch retargeting and email automation campaigns
* Reduce inefficient advertising spend
